Genetic Algorithm Optimization Engine

  • Balanced exploration and exploitation to ensure the model didn't get stuck in local optima.
  • Business Impact: Delivered an AI engine capable of finding near-optimal solutions to highly complex constraints in a fraction of the time of traditional computing methods, saving both time and computational overhead.
